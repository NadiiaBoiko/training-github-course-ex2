# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designers

### Role Summary
Focuses on user experience quality, interface design, and alignment with user research findings.

### Responsibilities
- Design wireframes, mockups, and user flows
- Conduct usability studies and collect user feedback
- Collaborate with Product Managers to clarify requirements
- Provide design assets and specifications for Developers
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Validate implementation matches design intent

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support adoption
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Partners with Product and Project Managers for requirements and priorities
- Works closely with Developers during implementation and design reviews
- Presents design rationale and user research findings
- Participates in sprint planning and demos

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
Responsible for deployment pipelines, CI/CD automation, infrastructure, monitoring, and reliability practices.

### Responsibilities
- Design, maintain, and optimize build and deployment pipelines
- Implement infrastructure-as-code and cloud resource management
- Monitor system health, alerting, and incident response
- Collaborate with Developers to integrate DevOps practices into workflows
- Manage environments (staging, production) and access controls
- Ensure security scanning and compliance in CI/CD

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, and repeatable deployments
- Minimize downtime and improve system reliability
- Automate toil and improve operational efficiency

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers and QA during release planning and incident resolution
- Supports Project Managers with deployment schedules and rollback procedures
- Provides infrastructure updates and capacity planning inputs
- Participates in post-incident retrospectives

---

## QA Automation Engineers

### Role Summary
Develop and maintain automated test suites to ensure product reliability, quality, and speed of delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain automated tests (unit, integration, end-to-end)
- Maintain test coverage metrics and report on quality status
- Work with Developers to improve code testability
- Identify, document, and track bugs and regressions
- Define and evolve testing strategies and frameworks
- Support continuous integration with automated test gates

### Goals
- Maximize test coverage and reduce manual testing burden
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Enable confident, rapid releases

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Developers to address defects rapidly and improve test infrastructure
- Supports Product Managers in defining testable acceptance criteria
- Reports on test results and quality metrics in sprint reviews
- Participates in planning to ensure testing effort is accounted for

---

## Business Analysts

### Role Summary
Translate business needs into actionable requirements and ensure solutions align with desired outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document requirements from stakeholders
- Analyze business processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Support Product Managers in roadmap and backlog refinement
- Facilitate communication between technical and non-technical teams
- Create process diagrams, user stories, and acceptance criteria
- Validate delivered solutions meet business objectives

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between business needs and technical delivery
- Reduce ambiguity in requirements and acceptance criteria
- Bridge communication gaps across diverse stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with Product Managers and stakeholders to elicit requirements
- Helps Project Managers clarify deliverable expectations and scope
- Presents requirements and process analysis in planning sessions
- Validates outcomes in sprint demos and reviews
