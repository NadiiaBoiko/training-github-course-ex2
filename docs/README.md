## Project Management Process Overview

OctoAcme uses a lightweight, iterative project management approach that balances structure with flexibility. The goal is to deliver customer value quickly and reliably while maintaining high quality and team wellbeing.

### Core Principles

- **Customer-first** — prioritize customer value and usability in every decision.
- **Iterative delivery** — deliver small, testable increments rather than large batches.
- **Clear ownership** — each project has a named Project Manager (PM) and Product Lead accountable for outcomes.
- **Data-informed decisions** — measure impact and iterate based on evidence, not assumptions.
- **Psychological safety** — encourage open feedback, learning from mistakes, and continuous improvement.

### Project Lifecycle

| Stage | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **1. Initiation** | Validate business need, align stakeholders, and create a lightweight plan with clear success criteria. |
| **2. Planning** | Break work into shippable increments, identify dependencies and risks, and agree on timelines and releases. |
| **3. Execution** | Build, test, review, and iterate with a regular team rhythm — standups, sprint reviews, and ongoing risk tracking. |
| **4. Release** | Deploy with proper verification steps, rollback plans, and stakeholder communication. |
| **5. Retrospective** | Capture learnings, celebrate wins, and drive actionable improvements for future cycles. |

### Key Roles

| Role | Responsibilities |
|---|---|
| **Project Manager (PM)** | Coordinates delivery, manages schedules, tracks risks, and facilitates team and stakeholder communication. |
| **Product Manager (PdM)** | Defines outcomes and success metrics, prioritizes the backlog, and validates solutions with users. |
| **Developers** | Implement features, collaborate on design and testability, and maintain code quality. |
| **QA / Testing** | Validate quality, define and verify acceptance criteria, and support release readiness. |
| **Stakeholders** | Provide strategic inputs, review progress, and give approvals at key decision gates. |

### Communication Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Daily standup | Daily (15 min) | Synchronize the delivery team, surface blockers. |
| PM + PdM sync | Weekly | Align on priorities, risks, and upcoming decisions. |
| Delivery sync | Weekly | Review progress against plan, update risk register. |
| Stakeholder update | Monthly | Communicate status, milestones, and upcoming releases. |
| Demo / Sprint review | End of each sprint or milestone | Demonstrate completed work and gather feedback. |
